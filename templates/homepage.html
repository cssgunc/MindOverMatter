About Us

Welcome to Mind Above Matter’s website!

Mind Above Matter is a nonprofit corporation that provides an online platform for college and university students to search for, learn about, and track upcoming events for the resources that best suit their particular mental health needs. Start by searching for mental health resources with our Search Bar  or by looking for one you already know with Resources! Under the Resources  section, you will find a complete list of all the on-campus mental health resources that UNC provides. Our Search Bar  has multiple tags and keywords to help you find the perfect resource for YOU! The Calendar  provides a day by day schedule of what mental health related events are at UNC.

For immediate help, see the Get Help Now  tab up above.

In an emergency, please call 911.
